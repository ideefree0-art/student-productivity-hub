Your Privacy Matters

At Student Productivity Hub, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at contact@studenthub.com.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register, use our services, or contact us. This can include:

We use your information to:

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
